HALAKKI VAKKALU

The Halakki-Vokkalu (also transliterated as Halwakki-Wakkal and Halakki-Vokkaliga are an indigenous tribe of Karnataka, India.They are found predominantly in Uttara Kannada district and are distinct from Vokkaligas. Halakki Vokkaligas living in the foot of Western Ghats are known as the "Aboriginals of Uttara Kannada".
